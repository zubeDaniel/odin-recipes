Jollof Recipe


Description

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and a variety of spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice in cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Add the onion and cook until translucent.
  4. Add the bell peppers and garlic, and cook for another minute.
  5. Stir in the spices and cook for 30 seconds.
  6. Add the tomatoes and broth, and bring to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  8. Stir in the mixed vegetables and cook for another 10 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite protein or as a side dish.
